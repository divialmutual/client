The roadmap to success

What are our major goals?

2020
March 2020 Concept development

Analysis of the situation of DeFi regarding the insurance that can be put in place. Creation of the team, and recruitment of a solidity developer.

Q3 & Q4 2020 First project prototype
  • MVP of wallet prototype released
  • Redesign of website goes live
  • Version 2 of Smart Contract released
  • Version 3 of Onepager released
2021
Q1 2021 Expansion

Massive recruitment of highly qualified people in the insurance field. Deployment of the first test contracts and analysis of our situation by organizations linked to life insurance management.

Q2 2021 Beta App User Testing

Release our first apps for beta testing to our members comunity

Q3 2021 Pre-Sale begin

Opening of the pre-sale for the purchase of tokens that will be used to earn on future transaction fees and subscriptions related to the Divial insurance.

Q4 2021 Audit

TechRate & Callisto audit.

Just to make sure everything is ok, our engineer are working hard on our smart contract but more audit = more security for our clients

2022
Q1 2022 Final contract deployment, public announcement of pools pricing

Creation of our own blockchain dedicated, creation of our own wallet. Deployment of 3 other smart contracts that were created to facilitate the migration to our own blockchain.

Q2 2022 "Deceased Transfer" protocol

Integration of a "Deceased Transfer" smart contract that allow a complete transfer of the funds if the owner (clients) die. A big step in DeFi insurance that no one want to miss !

Q3 2022 Final project

We assume that Divial project will be finish on the Q3 2022, all the pools will be ready to be use. Maintenance and update may occurs !
